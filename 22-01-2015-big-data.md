! Big Data  & NoSQL

Big data is about real-time/batch processing of 4Vs
* Volume, 
* Variety
* Velocity
* Veracity

NoSQL is about random-access of large amounts of data with trade-offs around
* Consistency
* Availability
* Parition Tolerance
